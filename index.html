Space facts

Space exploration has led to groundbreaking discoveries. Here's a few of them.

Galactic Cannibalism

The Milky Way is currently in the process of cannibalizing a smaller galaxy known as the Sagittarius Dwarf Spheroidal Galaxy.

Over time, the Milky Way will consume this galaxy entirely.

Supermassive Black Holes

At the center of most galaxies, including our own, lies a supermassive black hole. These giants can have masses billions of times that of our sun.

Space Agriculture

Did you know that we have grown and harvested lettuce in space? This research is crucial for sustaining future long-duration space missions.
